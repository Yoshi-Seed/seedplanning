Strategic Market Entry Insights: Oncology Therapeutics in Japan

Global Pharmaceutical Research Case Study

Executive Summary

We supported a major U.S. pharmaceutical company in navigating the complexities of the Japanese oncology market. By identifying critical differences in medical specialties (Organ-based vs. Disease-based) and insurance systems, we provided actionable insights for successful global expansion.

Key Differences Identified

Medical Specialty Japan: Organized by organ type
U.S.: Disease-based oncology
Pathology Complexity in genetic panel testing and reimbursement systems in Japan.

The Outcome

Seed Planning established a position as a trusted local partner, leading to continued support for positioning research in the next phase.
